PENBOM forged steel floating ball valves are designed in accordance with ISO17292 featuring 2 pc split body or 3 pc split body, ideal for medium or high pressure services and wide temperature range (-196℃~ 500℃) . It can substitute the small size trunnion mounted ball valves to save cost. PENBOM full range of forged steel floating ball valves is fire safe test certified by Llody’s Register per API607 and API6FA.
