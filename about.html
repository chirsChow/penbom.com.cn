Company Profile

PENBOM FLUID TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. is a developing and privately owned company based in Suzhou China. Specializing in manufacturing and stocking of both standard and customized valves. We offered all kinds of industrial valves to the worldwide Oil & Gas, E&P, Petrochemical, Chemical, Mining/Minerals, Power companies.

As established producers of high specification supplies and a vast range of in-house industry experience, PENBOM can offer an impressive turn-around on your most urgent of orders.

Our most comprehensive range sold and stocked are Pinch Valve, Diaphragm Valve, Butterfly and Knife Gate Valves, GGC, Ball Vavle. The materials can be Ductile Iron, Cast Iron, Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Duplex Stainless Steel.
